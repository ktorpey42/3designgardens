OUR DESIGN PROCESS

BRIEF

We begin with a site visit to get to know your requirements and familiarise ourselves with the garden. This initial meeting and discussion is the basis on which the design proposals are made and is known as the client brief.

DESIGN CONCEPT

We then produce a presentation of the proposed design. This includes photos, mood boards and drawings which will let you see how the finished garden will look. It is a discussion document which allows the design to change and evolve. We charge a fixed fee for the design concept.

PLANTING

We also produce a complete plant list to suit the design, aspect and soil characteristics of the garden. This includes the recommended sizes and location for each plant.

CONSTRUCTION

We have an association with a contractor with whom we have worked previously. They receive a full drawing package, bills of quantities and specifications. Following a site visit they provide an itemised quotation for your review - you may if you wish proceed with another contractor of your choice.
